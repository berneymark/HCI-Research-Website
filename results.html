Results Summary

Based on the results we got from the experiment most people that knew how to set it up were good at using the stylus. This will probably have to do with them having some sort of technical experience or expertise and vise versa for people who had a difficult time. The outliner in the experiment would be User 4 and User 6, as User 4 didn’t make any mistakes during the maze experiment and had a perfect signature, which means he must of used a stylus before in their life. Then for User 6 they had no idea how to set up or use the stylus, this user must have little to no technical experience or expertise or some sort of physical disability that makes their hand unstable for this to happen.

Based on the results we got from the interview most people found the stylus somewhat like the stylus and the comfort of it which tells me that the stylus itself is a good design for holding and writing with. Almost everyone thought there needed to be more space on the pad which tells me that if they gave more space for support then it would be better. The stylus weight was a mixed response across the board which makes me think that it’s a preference based utility and if the stylus had a way of adding and removing weight it would be a better product. In addition some people would had liked better grip to improve the pen.
